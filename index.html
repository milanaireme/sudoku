    Shortcuts

    Fast keyboard play

    • Arrow keys or W A S D move around the grid.
    • 1-9 fills a cell. 0, Delete, or Backspace clears it.
    • Shift + 1-9 freezes or unfreezes a digit. Enter or Space drops it into the selected cell.
    • N toggles notes. P clears the frozen digit. U undoes.
    • H hints, F toggles focus mode, C checks, G starts a new board, and B jumps focus back.
